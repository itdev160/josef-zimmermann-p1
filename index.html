Milwaukee County Beaches

Beaches:

Atwater Beach

This 5-acre open space features a sandy beach, public art, a playground & scenic lake views.

Address: 4000 N Lake Dr, Shorewood, WI 53211


Bradford Beach

Large public beach featuring yoga & crossfit classes, cabana rentals & beach volleyball courts.

Address: 2400 N Lincoln Memorial Dr, Milwaukee, WI 53202


South Shore Beach

Park on Lake Michigan with a beach, a beer garden, a playground & views of downtown.

Address: 2900 S Shore Dr, Milwaukee, WI 53207


Grant Park Beach

Pristine sandy lakeside beach with a snack bar, playground, volleyball court & kayak rental.

Address: Grant Park Beach, Wisconsin 53172
